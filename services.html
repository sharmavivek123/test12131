Our Services & Products

Gas & Oil Industries

In the oil & gas industry, the integrity of assets such as pipelines, storage tanks, mounded storage vessels, heat exchangers, and small vessels is crucial for operational efficiency and safety.

Water & Wastewater

Water treatment plants deal with constant exposure to harsh chemical environments, including chlorinated water, which can accelerate corrosion for metal components such as pipes, valves, tanks, and basins.

Marine & Offshore

Specialized cathodic protection solutions for offshore platforms, ship hulls, marine structures, and underwater pipelines in harsh marine environments.

Pipeline Integrity Surveys

Professional CIPL (Close Interval Potential Logging), CAT (Current Attenuation Test), DCVG (Direct Current Voltage Gradient), and Soil Resistivity surveys.

Monitoring & Maintenance

Regular inspections and maintenance ensure CP systems continue to function properly, preventing costly repairs, safety hazards, and operational downtime.

Cathodic Protection Technologies

TCP (Temporary Cathodic Protection) and ICCP (Impressed Current Cathodic Protection) systems with advanced monitoring and control technologies.
